Spooky Scary Skeleton

Spooky scary skeleton is the title of a 1996 children's halloween song by Andrew Gold. It appeared on the album Andrew Gold's halloween howls and has been presently made into a halloween costume to scare people.

PennyWise

Pennywise costume was originated from the movie "IT" Penny wears a light grey victorian-esque blouse with puff sleves with ruffles and holds a ballon to scare children which is now one of the costumes people wear for halloween

The Purge

The Purge is one of the most popular modern horror franchises and has made its mark due to its creepy costumes and distributing visuals.It is presently one out of the many others halloween costumes used by people during halloween.

Scary Nun

The Scary nun halloween costume was originated from the horror movie "The Nun" and became one of the halloween cosplay fancy dress.

Grim Reaper

Grim reaper is usually a skeletal figure who is often shrouded in a dark,hoodedrobe and carrying a scythe to reap human souls and presently people use his outfit as halloween costume
